From surface outcrops to subsurface certainty.

My journey in geology began with field mapping and evolved into understanding the subsurface through well log interpretation and stratigraphic analysis.

Today, I continue expanding my skills in GIS, mine planning, and geological modeling using ArcGIS, Minescape, Spry, and Global Mapper. I enjoy learning new tools and applying geological knowledge to solve practical challenges in both the energy and mining industries. Turning geological observation into engineering decisions, whether that's a stratigraphic column, a resource block model, or a contoured surface in ArcGIS.
